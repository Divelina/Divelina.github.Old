Why change my profession?

Of course, the reasons that lead me to consider another profession are complex. And still quite simple. Despite the title promising revelations, I am still not ready to share these reasons. I haven't yet gathered the mental power to write them down objectively. In fact, I haven't taken a decision yet. I'll play around with this blog and other tasks that the SoftUni courses offer and I'll see if I'll stay as enthusiastic about programming as I'm now.

Why programming?

At present, writing simple programs makes me eager to learn more and more. Maybe it's just the pleasure of getting some visible result with a very small effort. This leads to unjustified self-satisfaction and smugness. Maybe I won't feel the same way when I start to advance and the things become harder and more tedious. Still, I think that this is the first thing in many years that I'm doing just for myself and the sake of it and not to please or impress someone else.
